Preamble

I've always had a passion for making and modifying things. Back in the MySpace era, I fully customized my profile by modding the CSS—even though I didn’t know what CSS was back then! I also modded video games from as early as I can remember.

As a kid, I converted my old fixed-gear bicycle to an 18-speed. Converted an electric guitar to a piezo-acoustic so I could use nylon strings on it. At 14, I even swapped a more powerful engine into my car

Before I began working in tech, I thought I would become a CNC Programmer. I started attending an apprenticeship, where I learned the basics of G-code and manufactured my very first pieces.

  • 2022

    I began working at ExamRoom.AI as an Onboarding Agent, where I was surrounded by a lot of talented developers. Talking with them sparked my interest in web development.

    I quickly started learning how the web works—clients, servers, HTTP, databases, hosting, and more—and began creating my first personal projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

  • 2023

    I decided to go all in and learn Web Development full-time. Started by learning PHP and SQL as I decided to focus on the WordPress Stack.

    Worked on filling the knowledge gaps by starting ever more ambitious projects.

    got practical hands-on experience with hosting, Cpanel, SEO, Google Search Console, etc

    the major milestones of this year were the first projects I did for actual clients

  • 2024

    I delved deeply into WordPress custom theme creation, learning about block themes, setting up build processes and the latest best practices.

    I also decided to start my own web design agency

    the major milestones of this year were learning React and createing my very first Custom Blocks.

  • 2025

    The future looks bright! I plan to go all in on Custom Block creation and really master the process
