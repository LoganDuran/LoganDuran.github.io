Preamble

I've always had a passion for making and modifying things. Back in the MySpace era, I fully customized my profile by modding the CSS—even though I didn’t know what CSS was back then! I also modded video games from as early as I can remember.

As a kid, I converted my old fixed-gear bicycle to an 18-speed. At 14, I swapped a more powerful engine into my car, and it worked great. I even converted an electric guitar to a piezo-acoustic so I could use nylon strings on it.

Before I began working in tech, I thought I would become a CNC Programmer. I started attending an apprenticeship, where I learned the basics of G-code and manufactured my very first pieces.

  • 2022

    I began working at ExamRoom.AI as an Onboarding Agent, where I was surrounded by a lot of talented developers. Talking with them sparked my interest in web development.

    I quickly started learning how the web works—client, server, hosting, databases, and more—and began creating my first personal projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

  • 2023

    I decided to go all in and started learning Web Development full-time. Started learning PHP and SQL as I decided to focus on the WordPress Stack.

    Worked on filling the knowledge gaps by starting ever more ambitious projects

    got practical hands-on experience with hosting, Cpanel, SEO, Google Search Console, etc

    the major milestone of this year was the first projects I did for actual clients

  • 2024

    Began freelancing with WordPress, started making a full stack CRUD project utilizing PHP and MySQL. Continued improving my Frontend skills, in particular my CSS. Delved into the world of SEO

  • 2025

    Started utilizing React for WordPress custom block project.
